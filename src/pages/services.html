Dental prosthodontics

Fixed prosthodontics

If you have a damaged tooth, one or more teeth taken out, or you are simply not satisfied with the appearance, colour or shape of your natural teeth, we can correct and improve your smile with the help of dental crowns, bridges and veneers.

In the case of partially damaged natural crown, where conservative fillings cannot rectify the issue, the use of dental crowns allows for rehabilitation of the appearance and function of the affected tooth. For patients who are missing one or multiple teeth, but have adjacent teeth bordering the empty space, the application of custom dental bridges is advised.

Dental bridges and crowns range from ceramic (porcelain), metal, combined, and zirconia crowns.

Mobile prosthodontics

When patients are missing number of teeth in the upper or lower jaw and lack a sufficient number of abutments (teeth used to support the bridge), when their order is incompatible to the natural placement of the teeth, or simply in the case of a need for a more financially feasible alternative for dental correction, complete or partial dentures can be an affordable and appropriate choice

For the construction of partial dentures, it is necessary that the patient has a few remaining natural teeth, to witch the dentures can be fixed with the help of hooks or attachments.

Cosmetic dentistry

Dental veneers

Dental veneers are aesthetic solutions that allow for the correction of the shape, colour and improvement of the overall appearance of the teeth, thus giving our patients an aesthetically pleasing, Holliwood-style smile of their dreams, without substantial loss of the dental tissue.

Dental veneers are also used for the correction of gaps between two mostly frontal teeth ( Diastema mediana), thus avoiding unnecessary scraping of the teeth or construction of dental crowns.

Veneers made from dental porcelain are fabricated in a dental technician laboratory, and composite veneers can be directly built-up in the mouth in just one visit.

Dental cleaning and polishing

Dental cleaning and polishing is a painless procedure which refers to the removal of tartar, pigments and stains from the teeth, with the use of ultrasound, specialized pastes and brushes. This procedure is recommended to be practiced at least twice a year, depending on the type of the saliva of the patient, the way oral hygiene is practiced, and the quantity of tea, coffee and cigarettes consumed by the patient.

Dental scaling

Dental scaling is a deep cleaning method for additional polishing of the teeth which have already been cleaned with an ultrasound. This is achieved with a dental scale which is using fine dental sand with different flavours, which leads to a meticulous finish and leaves the dental enamel brightened, shinier and healthier.

Given the expertise of Dr Josif in this area, we warmly recommend that patients looking for cleaning and whitening of their teeth, try dental scaling for best results.
