Our Mission
Raising the level of dental health care and working with a different approach while providing dental services to our patients, enabling them a pleasant experience when coming in our dental clinic and satisfaction of their own oral health that we are improving to an optimal function and esthetics, all done by professionals with the latest technologies, in great environment.

These are our words, that can describe the best who are we and our dental office guaranteed quality, safety, trust and satisfaction. At our office there is a unwritten rule that says: ,,Once you are our patient, forever be our patient and be the part of our big family of satisfied patients and friends of the dental office Mak Dental - Bitola.”





Our History
The dental office “Mak Dental - Bitola” is officially opened on 15.07.2019 in Bitola. Previously, in a period of two years, a complete adaptation and reconstruction of the office was made. Today, the office space is completely adapted and meets all the requirements from the Ministry of Health of the Republic of Macedonia. The space itself is designed by the highest standards, with the possibility for enlargement of the clinic in the fields of dental prosthetics, dental implants and dental radiology. We also use sophisticated dental equipment and materials from renowned world manufacturers while providing our services to our patients.

In our dental office, from the opening until today, we’ve been providing our services to many satisfied patients, most of them coming from the Pelagonia Region, as well as other parts of the country, and patients from abroad who have not accidentally chosen us to take care of their oral health.

Dr. Josif Ristevski

Founder and chief dentist

Born in Bitola on 08.25.1988. He completed his primary school in his hometown of Demir Hisar and Medical High School, at the Department of Dental Technicians, in Bitola, in 2006/2007. He successfully completed his studies at the Faculty of Dentistry at The Ss.Cyril and Methodius University in Skopje, in 2012.

In 2010, as a student, he won his first prize at the International Scientific Congress of Dentistry Students, in Ohrid on the topic of oral presentation (Total prosthesis, Diagnosis problems and Treatment plan under the mentorship of Ass.Dr. Blagoja Dashtevski (associates Ass.Dr. Vancho Spirov and Ass.Dr. Edward Janev).

In 2011, as a graduate of the Faculty of Dentistry, he participated in the International Dental Congress in Hvar, Croatia with his own paperwork (Classical and metal-ceramic fixed constructions - advantages and disadvantages - Mentor Ass.Dr. Marjan Petkov).

After graduation, he used to live in Zagreb, Croatia to pursue private practise, as well as to complete part of his mandatory internship in private clinics and dental laboratories, such as ANDI DENT Dental Clinic and Dental Technical Laboratory, Dr. Gashparac and others. During his stay in Zagreb, Dr. Ristevski notarized his diploma in order to be able to further develop professionally in the field of dentistry in the Republic of Croatia.

Dr. Ristevski has participated in numerous seminars and has conducted many trainings in the field of dental Prosthetics (Duceram kiss ceramic), Implantology (Ancilos implantology-Promedika and Ohrid-Vezika), Aesthetic dentistry (Lumineri), Oral surgery (Ivoclar Vivadent, Belgrade, Serbia), Maxillofacial Surgery (Skopje EACMFS educational center), Balcan Stomatological Educational Congress, and etc.

To this day, Dr. Ristevsi continues with his education and cooperation with his colleagues from Republic of Macedonia and Zagreb, Croatia.

Dobre Ristevski

Dental technician

Over 40 years of working experience. His first employment was in 1978, in the Health Center in Demir Hisar. Immediately after graduation from the Medical High School Dr. Panche Karagjozov in Skopje, where he worked in the Department of Dental Prosthetics until 2006. After 2006 he continue to work as a private dental technician, using the same laboratory as a concessionaire. Since 2017, he has been engaged in our dental office as a professional associate and dental technician until this day. He has visited and participated in a number of professional seminars.

When it comes to dental technicians, our dental office also cooperates with other dental laboratories from Bitola and Zagreb, Croatia.
